\nMeeting will take place on 15th October in the Fletcher Room, Trinity
\nCollege, Oxford. The day is scheduled to begin at around 10:30 and to
\nrun until around 16:00. The UK-TUG AGM will take place as part of the
\nday: see the separate notice.<\/p>\n

Talks on TeX-related are invited: talk times are flexible.<\/p>\n

The committee has invited a special guest: Stefan Kottwitz of Lufthansa Industry Solutions. He will talk about applications of TeX in industry, such as using TeX for programming Cisco switches and generating converged network designs.<\/p>\n

Attendance at the meeting is free for members of UK-TUG. The committee
