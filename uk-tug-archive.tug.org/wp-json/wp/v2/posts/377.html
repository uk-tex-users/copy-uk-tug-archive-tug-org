Come to Summer 2009 – the UK’s Open Systems Conference – where both
\ndevelopers and users of Free Libre and Open Source get together to
\nswap knowledge and learn about the latest happenings in the Free
\nSoftware world.<\/p>\n

The venue is the The Conservatoire, Birmingham City University, B3
\n3HG, England. The Conservatoire is in the centre of Britain’s Second
\nCity within walking distance of major train and coach stations and
\nhotels at all price ranges.<\/p>\n

UKUUG conferences have the highest signal to noise ratio of the UK’s
\nopen system conferences. As well as useful talks and tutorials there
\nis a healthy social scene outside the talk sessions where you will
\nmake new friends as well as renew old acquaintances. With a central
\nlocation in the centre of the country it is really easy to get to and
\naccommodation at all price levels is available within a short walk of
\nthe conference venue.<\/p>\n

With special rates for students everyone can afford to go, so mark
\nSummer 2009 in your diaries now! Full details are at:
