In the UK-TUG inbox today we received a message about an upcoming meeting at the Open University in Milton Keynes focussed on maths in electronic media.<\/p>\n

Hello<\/p>\n

We’re pleased to announce that there will be a one-day technical workshop on mathematical content in electronic media, to take place at The Open University, Milton Keynes on Wednesday 9th September.<\/p>\n

The workshop will have three main themes:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. Content related technical problems in supporting e-Learning in mathematics<\/li>\n
  2. Standards related to digitisation of mathematics research literature<\/li>\n
  3. Formulas and equations in otherwise non-mathematical content<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    Attendance is limited to about 30, and there are funds for travel and accommodation.<\/p>\n

    We thank JISC and the OU for funding this workshop.<\/p>\n

    For more information, and to let us know you’d like to attend, visit:
