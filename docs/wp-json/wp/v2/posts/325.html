Niall Mansfield has been in touch with the Committee, looking for some input on the potential for getting a number of LaTeX books in German translated in English. The general topics of the books are:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. LaTeX reference<\/li>\n
  2. Maths with LaTeX<\/li>\n
  3. Tables with LaTeX<\/li>\n
  4. PSTricks<\/li>\n
  5. KOMA-Script<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    Niall is hoping to to get input into three general questions:<\/p>\n

      \n
    1. Is this a useful book for the (La)TeX world?<\/li>\n
    2. What sort of demand would there be? (This isn’t scientific: \u201cwow, lots of people would need this\u201d or \u201cgreat book, but nobody is likely to buy it because \u2026\u201d is the sort of granularity that’s OK.)<\/li>\n
    3. How long would this book last before a new edition is likely to be:\n
        \n
      • desirable<\/li>\n
      • essential<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

        After some discussion with the Committee, Niall has agree to take the books along to the regular The Tech Pub Crawl<\/a> this Tuesday (the 5th of May). Niall will be at the The Marketplace Bar, 11 Marketplace, London W1W 8AH<\/a> from about 6 p.m. (nearest tube Oxford Circus).
