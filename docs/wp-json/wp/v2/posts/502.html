There will be elections for Chair and Committee later this year. After four years as Chair Jonathan Fine intends to stand down, as does our Treasurer, David Saunders.<\/p>\n

The committee has not yet made arrangements for these elections, but closing dates for nominations will be no earlier than mid-September. So if you’d like to contribute to TeX in the UK as Chair or Committee member, please consider standing.<\/p>\n

We’re thinking of holding the AGM as part of the FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) Unconference<\/a> in Birmingham, which takes place on Saturday 16th October.<\/p>\n

The venue is the classic Birmingham and Midland Institute<\/em><\/a>, in the city centre, just a couple of minutes from the Art Gallery and Museum, which is running an exhibition on the spectacular Anglo-Saxon Staffordshire hoard<\/a> of gold and silver jewellery.<\/p>\n

So please do consider standing for the committee, and let us know<\/a> how you feel about our plans for the AGM.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
