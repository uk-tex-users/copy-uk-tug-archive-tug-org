In the UK-TUG inbox recently was a mail from Karl Berry (TUG President) with information about upcoming deadlines for TUG 2009 in Notre Dame, Indiana. Karl said:<\/p>\n

TUG 2009 will take place in Notre Dame, Indiana, from July 28\u201331. See http:\/\/tug.org\/tug2009<\/a> for the registration form, maps, the proposals already accepted, and more.<\/p>\n

April has several deadlines related to the conference:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • April 13: deadline for abstract submissions; see
    \n    http:\/\/tug.org\/tug2009\/cfp.html<\/a> for the call for papers.Although we may be able to accept proposals after the deadline, of course potential attendees would like to know what they’ll be seeing \ud83d\ude42 <\/span><\/span>. So if you’d like to give a talk, please try to submit an abstract by the 13th.<\/li>\n
  • April 17: deadline for bursary applications; see
    \n    http:\/\/tug.org\/bursary<\/a> for information and the application form. No late applications will be accepted.<\/li>\n
  • April 27: deadline for the early bird registration discount. After this, the registration fee will be increased. Register for the conference at http:\/\/tug.org\/tug2009\/register.html<\/a> (it will redirect you to Notre Dame’s web site).<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
